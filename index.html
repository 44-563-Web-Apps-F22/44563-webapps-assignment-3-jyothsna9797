Shankar’s International Dolls Museum Delhi

It is one is one of the most renowned museums in New Delhi which has a huge collections of dolls from across the globe.
It was founded by the renowned political cartoonist, K. Shankar Pillai in 1965 with thousand dolls.
It has around 6000 Dolls from 85 countries across the globe.

Directions to reach Museum:

Closest Airport: Indira Gandhi International Airport, New Delhi, via NH 8

  1. It is located on 4 Bahadur Shah Zafar Marg inside the Children’s Book Trust in Nehru House.
  2. We can reach the museum through various modes of transport:
    1. Metro
    2. City Buses
    3. Private Cabs
  3. I have been there via Metro by the following directions.
    1. From the I.G.I. Airport station, take the Orange Line to New Delhi Metro station.
    2. Then change over to the Yellow Line to Central Secretariat.
    3. Next switch to the Violet Line to ITO station.
    4. Finally, from there the museum is only a couple of minutes’ walk from there.
  4. Note: Delhi in Metro is Most expensive can get there by other ways by city buses probably will get you there with cheaper cost.
